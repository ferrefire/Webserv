ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

TrainingAir is an independent EASA Approved Training Organisation (DK/ATO/030), approved to conduct licence proficiency checks, instructor courses and type ratings for the Airbus A320, A330, A340 and B737 NG to individuals and airlines, at training centres in Europe & Asia.

TrainingAir was founded in 2012 by Arne Lund Pedersen and Morten Thvilum, two experienced Danish Training Captains and Examiners, to provide the highest quality training experience possible pilot community, in Scandinavia and across the globe.
